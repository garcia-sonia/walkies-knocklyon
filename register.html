Donate

Go Fund Me is the easy way to raise funds for WALKIES - Knocklyon. Make direct donations or create fundraising pages. We need your help to keep our Knocklyon seniors and their pets together.♡

Volunteer

As a charity, WALKIES - Knocklyon is supported by the efforts of our dedicated volunteers. We greatly appreciate your willingness to join us and donate your time. Please register using the above form letting us know your availabilty.

Adopt

WALKIES - Knocklyon works in partnership with our local animal shelter and can help you connect with a rescue dog. Our adoption programme will fund any adoption fees and also includes financial support for vet fees and pet supplies. Please register using the above form.
